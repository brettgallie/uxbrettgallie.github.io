Ar/VR Insuretch App Concept

1
The UX Challenge

Startup Bootcamp (Insurtech) tasked our team to come up with an augmented reality app that would increase customer engagement through gamification.

2
The UX Approach

As the Lead UX Designer I constructed a basic storyboard for the app and as a team we chose a Harry Potter theme for our augmented reality app.

3
The Final product

Using Just-In-Mind I designed a basic walk through of the app and ran an A-Frame web virtual reality design sprint while our developers worked on building the main app on the Unity platform.

4
The UX Impact

We presented the final product to the Insurance Companies and they are using the results of our design sprints in the development of their own products.
