AI and iPad App Concept

1
The UX Challenge

Near Earth Objects (NEO’s) can be lost in the mountains of data that is collected by NASA. We had the task of using artificial intelligence tools combined with UX solutions to resolve this issue.

2
The UX Approach

Our challenge was to develop a machine learning solution and or app to help track these hazards.As UX designer I prototyped an iPad app which global observers could report near earth objects to NASA, , and prototyped an AI solution using Google’s Deepmind machine learning algorithms to assist in NEO asteroid tracking

3
The Final product

I produced a MVP iPad app and a Business Requirements Document containing my research into Google Deepmind and its application in the search for Near Earth Objects.

4
The UX Impact

This project is ongoing and I have participated in numerous international Asteroid Day events.
