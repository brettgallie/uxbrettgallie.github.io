Our team built a simulator based on the project brief. We filtered out the signal from all of the noise in the data. We synthesised the Space Data with the Socio- Economic Data to build our Simulator. I led the dev team using my UX and Data visualisation knowledge to guide the project.

How We Addressed This Challenge

We used the Boardgame "Pandemic" which inspired us to build a simulator. We used one of the Members Twitter Data to build a rough timeline of how the Pandemic Spread. As the Data sample was too large we narrowed our focus to the EMEA region - with data samples from Italy and Spain We used Agile Methodology and the latest Software Tools to iterate our Simulation (Trello, Github, Slack and Zoom Meetings in addition to the Space Apps Chat Channels and spoke to our NASA mentors)
